    Rules summary

    • There are 25 words and 15 agents that both players must contact together.
    • Place a paper screen on the center line, then reveal both opposite-facing key sides for players seated across from each other.
    • In multi-device mode, scan Side A and Side B QR codes to show private keys on separate phones.
    • Each clue-giver privately sees nine green agents and three black assassins on their key side.
    • Give a one-word clue and a number. Your partner guesses words linked to that clue.
    • A green guess is contacted and guessing may continue. A bystander ends the turn. An assassin loses immediately.
    • You spend one timer token at the end of each normal turn. After the last token, sudden death begins.
    • In sudden death no clues are given: contact all remaining agents, but any non-agent guess loses.

    Based on the standard cooperative rules in the official Codenames: Duet rulebook.
